ABOUT ME

SABRINA SAMUEL

I'm based in London, UK. Since I bought my camera, in January 2020, I've tried to take pictures of things that interest me. Whether they are Red Pandas or sporting events, I've tried to get as many shots as I can. While the pandemic has limited some of the places/events I had planned, I've still managed to take the odd one when I can. I still need to learn how to get the best out of my camera. Everyday is a work in progress.
